Accessibility Matters

Creating digital and physical spaces for everyone.

Why Accessibility Matters

  • Promotes inclusion and equal opportunity.
  • Removes barriers in education, work, and daily life.
  • Supports independence and dignity.
  • Benefits everyone through better design.

Accessibility Facts

Over 1 billion people worldwide live with some form of disability.

Many websites still fail basic accessibility standards.

Captions, ramps, and readable text help everyone—not only disabled users.

Accessible Web Design Tips

  • Use alt text for images.
  • Ensure strong color contrast.
  • Allow keyboard navigation.
  • Add captions to videos.
  • Use clear headings and readable fonts.
  • Make websites mobile responsive.
